About Our Firm


Servet Wealth Management was built around the idea that business, finances and personal life all tend to blend together. Our founder Nathan created Servet Wealth Management with these principals in mind. Nathan has a multi-cultural family and wanted a name to reflect that. The name Servet is a node to his wife’s Turkish culture as it is a word that means fortune, and just as importantly, its English root word is serve. Creating a business that was rooted in serving client’s wealth was a fundamental principal and something we strive for everyday.



Prior to starting Servet Wealth Management, I began my career in 2012 at Merrill Lynch where I built a successful practice helping clients meet their financial goals. I got my CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER certificate in 2019 so I could help clients through more complex financial planning scenarios. This is what I enjoy most about my job; taking real world challenges and finding solutions that clients can feel confident in implementing.


Personally, I grew up camping and hiking our National Parks and count camping in Yosemite Valley amongst my favorite memories. When my wife and I travel to new cities, we enjoy renting bikes and riding around the city finding under-the-radar restaurants.
